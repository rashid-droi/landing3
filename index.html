Frequently asked questions

How does this course work?

Once you enroll, you will be given login details to the learning platform where you can access the course materials. You can then study at your own pace, anytime, anywhere on any device as long as you are connected to the internet. The access to the platform will be valid for 1 month, which you can renew on a monthly basis, though one month is more than enough to complete the content of one course. You can also get extended access, and save from paying monthly subscription if you buy the course in a package. Check the packages here.

Will I get a certificate after this course?

Yes, you will receive a certificate of completion after you complete the course with at least 80% success rate.

What if I don't pass the course?

In each module of the course, there are quizzes to check your knowledge. You will be given multiple attempts to pass the course. If you don’t pass the first time, you can review the course and do the quizzes again.

What should I do if the name on my certificate is wrong?

Please make sure you enter your personal details correctly when registering. If you have submitted wrong details, email us at hello@selectgroup.ae. We will send the certificate with the correct details.

Can I take several courses?

Of course you can. If you buy courses in a package, you will get a discounted rate and longer access period. Check the packages here.

How often are courses updated?

All courses are regularly updated to ensure the content is current and relevant. Plus, we will keep on adding new courses to the platform to make sure we cater to our client needs based on industry trends and requirements.

How to participate in other learning events?

We conduct various training activities online and face to face. You can learn about upcoming events on our website or social media pages. Follow us on social media here.

What should I do if I have a technical problem?

We’re here to help you! Simply send us an email at hello@selectgroup.ae and our support team will resolve the issue.

Can I get a separate invoice if my employer pays for the course?

Sure! Many of our learners are sponsored by their organisation. Simply email us at hello@selectgroup.ae with your request.

How long do I have access to the class videos?

If you buy one course, you will be given a 1 month access to the platform, which you can renew on a monthly basis. Also you can buy courses in a package, and get a discounted rate and longer access period. Check the packages here.
